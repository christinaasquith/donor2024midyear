Hack Club Midyear 2024 Report to Donors

X teenagers taking on technical challenges:

For years, Hack Club has been an underground movement of several thousand teen hackers, but this month we launched our summer challenge the “Hack Club Arcade”in partnership with big names: GitHub, Minecraft Education, FIRST Robotics, and 10 other tech companies and nonprofit coding clubs. We expect 10k students to participate; with 500-1,000 rising to the challenge of completing a successful challenge, from graphics programming to program language development, AI and Ham radio. Within the first hour, teenagers from 60 different countries had started.

X friendships made through Wholesome, in-person social hackathons:

Highly technical teenagers often feel isolated, and so each year, Hack Club runs in-person hackathons to bring young people together in healthy, wholesome spaces where they can code, and also gain agency, leadership and friendships.
